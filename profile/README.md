## What is BookBy?

BookBy connects clients with beauty masters — barbers, nail artists, massage therapists and more. No more DMs in Telegram or Instagram. Just open the app, find a master nearby, and book a slot.

## Two apps

| | BookBy for Clients | BookBy for Masters |
|---|---|---|
| **Who** | Anyone looking for a beauty service | Masters, barbers, salons |
| **Key feature** | Find & book in 30 seconds | Manage schedule & clients |
| **Price** | Free forever | Free trial 2 months, then from 20 BYN/mo |

## Repositories

| Repo | Description |
